Howard residences reflect the diversity of our students.

Howard University has nine Residence Halls on campus and 2 Residence Halls off-campus in private apartment complexes. Each residence hall has 24/7 controlled entry, laundry facilities, and other amenities. The halls are managed by our Campus Apartments and WISH partners with additional facilities support from REDCAM.

Traditionally, our first-year residence halls are single-gender identity and our continuing residence halls are available for all genders. Our current housing policy guarantees housing for all 1st-year freshmen and 2nd-year sophomores. Transfer students are prioritized each semester but must confirm housing availability at the time of receiving enrollment confirmation as beds may be sold out closer to the semester's start. 

Each hall includes programming, helpful resources, and visitation during approved times. The hours for visitation are 12 p.m. - 11:59 p.m. Sunday through Thursday and 12 p.m. - 12:59 a.m. on Friday and Saturday. Visitation typically opens one week after the close of registration each semester and is only open to Howard University students. 

 

Looking for Summer School Housing 2024? Complete the summer housing form https://forms.office.com/r/fTPdPijBu5 . Summer housing is reserved only for Howard University students taking summer classes at the University. 

Always contact your Resident Assistant, Graduate Assistant, and Building Manager first. They can answer your questions most efficiently.

Residence Hall Style Definition

All of our residence halls are primarily described by the style listed below.
Type Description
Unit Style This is a one room design that is typical to a "dorm" room. Students in these will either have their own space or share with a roommate in this one room setup.
Suite Style This is a multi-room design where multiple rooms are connected by a common area space or hallway. Suites range from having 2 to 4 rooms which may include single rooms and/or double rooms.
Apartment Style This is a multi-room design that is typical to an apartment. These have different rooms connected in a full apartment with living room and kitchen. These have either single rooms or double rooms.

Bathroom Type Definition

Type Description
No Bath This type only has access to a communal bathroom typically located on every floor. This is in unit style only.
Communal Bath A communal bathroom is a shared bathroom with toilet and shower stalls. These are open 24/7 for resident use. Guests of other gender within the hall must use the guests bathroom located in the lobby.
Shared Bath This type has a bathroom connected to the room and other rooms. This is in unit style and suite style.
Full Bath This type has access to a dedicated bathroom which is connected. This is in unit, suite, and apartment style.

Room Type Description

What is the difference between a double, standard double, and large studio double? Review below to find out!
Type Description
Standard Single This is a single occupancy room without additional features in the unit. These are in unit style only.
Single This is a single occupancy room which have additional features such as kitchenettes or full kitchens.
Single with Full Bath, Mini-Kitchen This is a single occupancy room with connected bathroom and small kitchenette area with cabinets, shelves, and sink.
Two Person Suite Single This is a single occupancy room with a shared bathroom. This suite style has two single rooms connected by small hallway or common area which leads to the shared bathroom and full kitchen.
One Bedroom This is a single occupancy bedroom with a connected bathroom and a separated living room space.
Standard Double This is a double occupancy room without additional features in the unit. These are in unit style only, suite style, and apartment style.
Double This is a double occupancy room which have additional features such as kitchenettes or full kitchens.
Small Double This is a double occupancy room which have additional features such as kitchenettes or full kitchens but are smaller in size. These are a great way to have access to additional features while saving money.
Large Studio Double, XL Room This is a double occupancy room with a connected bathroom that is shared between the two residents. This room is an extra large double with additional space and full kitchen.
Double Deluxe, Shared Bath, XL Room This is a double occupancy room with a connected bathroom that is shared between the two residents and a kitchenette area. This room is an extra large double with additional space.
